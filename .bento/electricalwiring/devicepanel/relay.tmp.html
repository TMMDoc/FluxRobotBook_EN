Relay Model: Phoenix Contact – 2961105 (Pic 8.1)

  • Type:

o 24v DC relay

  • Quantity:

o 6 Relays

  • Purpose:

o Used for switching between different circuits, allowing a 24v DC control signal to operate the relay, this in turn controls a higher voltage or current circuit.

Relay Socket Model: Phoenix Contact – PLC-BSC-24DC/21

  • Quantity:

o Corresponding to the number of relays used (6 units).

  • Purpose:

o Socket is designed to securely hold and electrically connect the relay to the control circuit.

o It provides the necessary connection points for both the relay’s control coil and switching contacts.

Socket connection points (Pic 8.2):

  1. A2 (-): Negative terminal for the relay coil.

  2. A1 (+): Positive terminal for the relay coil.

  3. COM (11/13): Common terminal for the switching contacts (Connected to NO or NC).

  4. NO (14): Normally open terminal, connects to COM when relay is energized.

  5. NC (12): Normally closed terminal, connects to COM when relay is de-energized.
