Terminals (Pic 7.1):

  • 6 terminals for 24v (Positive)

  • 6 terminals for 0v (Negative)

  • Terminals are short linked within their respective groups (24v and 0v)

Separator:

  • Block separator is placed between the 24v and 0v terminals to prevent direct electrical contact.

Voltage slots:

  • 12 slots for each Positive and Negative with 1 as main supply.

  • Remaining 11 slots are free to use for various connections

Purpose:

  • This setup provides organized distribution of power to multiple devices or components.

  • The shared ground (24v) and (0v) terminals allow for flexible and efficient connection to the power supply.
