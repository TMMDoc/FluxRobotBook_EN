Input terminals (Pic 6.1):

  • 20 sets of input terminals arranged in two decks namely upper and lower with 10 terminals in each (Pic 6.2).

Functions:

  • Serves as an intermediary between sensors and the robot control terminal.

Key benefits:

  • Cable management:

o Simplifies the organization and routing of cables to the robot controller.

  • Modularity:

o Easy addition of new sensors or external devices.

o Quick removal of devices or sensors when no longer needed.
